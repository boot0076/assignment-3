About Us

The City of Ottawa's Community Pool is the best place to relax and take advantage of the health benefits which come along with aquatics. Whether you prefer instructional programs, leisure swim, aquatic leadership or permitting a pool, we have something safe for everyone. Swimming is a great way to maintain lifelong fitness and wellness as it is one of the best forms of exercise for its aerobic and resistance training benefits.
